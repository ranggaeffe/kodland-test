Do You Know about POP IT ?

A Pop-It (also known as Go Pop and Last One Lost) is a fidget toy consisting of a usually-brightly colored silicone tray with pokable bubbles, similar to bubble wrap, that can be flipped and re-used. They come in a variety of colors, shapes, and sizes, and are marketed as a stress-reliever.

Why is Pop It so popular?

Fidget toys, in general, help kids release nervous or restless energy, Dr. Sanam Hafeez, a New York City-based neuropsychologist tells InsideHook. And kids could be subconsciously using the Pop It! as a stress reliever. “Popping the little bubbles may also relax muscle tension, reduce anxiety and stress,” she says
